USA H1B Work Visa

The H-1B is a non-immigrant visa that allows U.S. companies to employ foreign workers in specialty occupations that require theoretical or technical expertise. The visa is valid for three years, extendable to six years, and often leads to a path for permanent residency. It requires employer sponsorship and is subject to an annual cap.
